Welcome to Moradabad


Moradabad, nestled on the banks of the picturesque Ramganga River in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, is a city that resonates with a rich cultural and industrial heritage. Known as the "Brass City" or "Peetal Nagri," Moradabad boasts a longstanding tradition of exquisite brass craftsmanship. Its bustling streets are adorned with an array of brassware, from intricately designed artifacts to utilitarian items, making it a hub for the production and export of fine brass products. Beyond its thriving industrial landscape, Moradabad is steeped in history, evident in its mosques, temples, and architectural landmarks that tell tales of bygone eras. The city is well-connected by road and rail, with a prominent railway junction facilitating easy access.
